Pallet Workbench

Background

While the majority of my projects are heavily design-focused, typically with a mechanism designed to accomplish a unique purpose, this project instead was instead intended to replicate a table design by using discarded wooden shipping pallets. I contacted a few businesses local to our area which regularly received shipments, and they were happy to let me take and use what otherwise would have ended up in a landfill. I spent some time sorting through several and selected two which were dry, heat treated, and in good condition.

Material Preparation

To prepare the pallet wood for processing, I first ripped them down using a pry bar and hammer. I removed all the nails and separated out the several boards. I then laid out the pieces to plan the layout to form the table.
