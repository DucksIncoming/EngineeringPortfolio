Projects

Long before I had any formal education in programming or electronics, I learned a wide range of skills and found new tools through my personal projects. Far beyond anything I've learned in a classroom, the tools and workflow I've developed for myself through rigorously pursuing projects that captivated me are what have ultimately shaped my identity and my goals for the future.

I learned how to program by developing games and desktop applications, and I've been making websites like this one for as long as I can remember (though, less certainly pretty). I managed to turn my zero working knowledge of Arduino and electronics into multiple usable devices, including an entire home security system, a wearable morse-code translation device, and even a chess match with AI-controlled tazers to punish mistakes!

If you want to learn more about any specific projects I've worked on in the past, several are documented below by category. If you have any questions about a specific project, please feel free to email me or check out the source code on my GitHub.
