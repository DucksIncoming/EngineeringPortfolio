About Me

Hi, My name is Ricky Jones, and I'm a mechanical engineering student, with a concentration in nuclear engineering and a minor in aerospace engineering. I'm very passionate about programming, 3D design, music production, and game development.

I'm currently a student in the Honors College at Virginia Commonwealth University, expecting to graduate in May 2027. I'm deeply passionate about establishing nuclear energy as a dominant power source for its reliability, safety, and renewability, and I hope to use my education in the future to help make that transition a reality. I'm also very passionate about the applications of nuclear energy in the aerospace industry, and I hope to contribute to the exploration and colonization of distant worlds in my future career. Beyond those ideals, I'm especially motivated by design work, as it combines complex problem solving with both aethstetic and functional design challenges.

This website is intended to be an easy to use online alternative for my resume, mixed with a "mini-portfolio", which goes over my experience, education, projects, and more. Please feel free to look around and contact me about anything you find interesting or have questions about. Thank you!
